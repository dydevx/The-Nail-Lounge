# Product

## Register

brand

## Users

Customers browsing The Nail Lounge services, checking prices, and booking salon appointments.

## Product Purpose

Present the salon, treatment menu, prices, contact details, and a clear route to online booking.

## Brand Personality

Refined, warm, and relaxing. The experience should feel welcoming and trustworthy.

## Anti-references

Avoid cluttered, overly colourful, visually noisy, or generic template-like salon websites.

## Design Principles

- Make services and prices quick to scan.
- Keep booking actions obvious and easy to reach.
- Use calm visual hierarchy that reflects the in-salon experience.
- Preserve clear responsive layouts across desktop and mobile.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

Keep text legible, controls keyboard accessible, and respect reduced-motion preferences.
