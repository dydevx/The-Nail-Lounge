About Us

Modern nail care with a calm, polished touch.

The Nail Lounge is a refined nail salon in Chipping Barnet created for clients who appreciate beautiful details, relaxed service and consistently elegant results. Our team brings together professional nail care, friendly attention, a soothing environment and high quality products for every appointment.
